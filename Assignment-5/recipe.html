Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook pasta.
  2. Heat olive oil and add sauce.
  3. Mix pasta and sauce together.
  4. Serve hot.

Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and lemon juice

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Add salt and lemon juice.
  3. Mix well and serve.

Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp tea leaves
  • Sugar to taste
  • Milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add tea leaves and sugar.
  3. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add milk if desired, then serve.
